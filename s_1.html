Semiya Upma Recipe

Ingrediants Required

vermicelli (sevaiya, semiya ) 1cup Oil :5 tsp. , Mustard seeds: 1tsp , Urad dal :½ tsp , Chana dal :1 tsp , Dried red chilli :1 , Curry leaves :few (4-5 ) , Peanuts :handful (10-15) , Ginger :1/2 ( finely chopped ) , Green chillies : 2 (slit in 2 pieces ) , Onion : 2 tbsp (finely chopped ) , Carrot :2 tbsp ( finely chopped ) , Turmeric: ½ tbsp, Salt: ½ tbsp, Coriander :2 tbsp (finely chopped ,

Procedure

1.On a pan put 2 tbsp of oil and one cup of vermicelli into it 2.on a low fame roast it until it becomes golden brown 3.after ,allow it to get completely cool 4.in a different utensil boil some water. Later as soon as water gets partiallty boiled put the roasted vermicelli into the boiling water along put ½ tbsp. of salt into the boiling water 5. mix the salt water and vermicelli thoroughly and allow it boil for 3 mins. 6. after 3 min. drain the water from the the untensil with the vermicelli remained into the untensil . 7. now take the pan used for roasting (or other) And put three tbsp. of oil into it .add 1 tbsp of mustard seeds half tbsp. of urad
